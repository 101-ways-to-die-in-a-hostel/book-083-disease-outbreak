# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 083
**Title:** Disease Outbreak
**Slug:** disease-outbreak
**Category:** Circumstantial/Random

## The Death (One Sentence)
A medical student who dismissed early symptoms as travel fatigue becomes patient zero in a hostel pandemic outbreak, dying alone in quarantine after infecting dozens.

## Tagline
"She thought she knew better. The virus disagreed."

## Themes
1. The arrogance of knowledge without wisdom (satirizes those who think expertise makes them immune to danger)
2. The petri dish of international travel (hostels as disease incubators)
3. The randomness of who lives and who dies in an outbreak

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 25%
- Horror: 50%
- Satire: 25%

## Target Reader
Anyone who has powered through sickness while traveling, ignored early symptoms, or stayed at a hostel during cold/flu season watching illness spread through dorms.

## Comparison Titles
- Contagion (2011) meets The Beach (2000)

- Pandemic themes (may trigger post-COVID anxiety)
- Isolation
- Multiple deaths
